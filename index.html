What is Budgeting?

Budgeting is simply creating a plan for your money. It's about knowing how much money you have coming in (income) and how much is going out (expenses).

By understanding your cash flow, you gain control over your finances, make informed spending decisions, and work towards your financial goals, like building your savings or paying off debt.

With the BudgetQuest app, you'll visualize your financial progress as your financial city grows!

You'll also have access to basic metrics to track your progress.
